Introduction

Yiwu Yao is a medicinal chemist, drug discoverer who is passionate about developing novel small molecules targeting oncoprotein. He is also a skillful organic chemist experienced in the total synthesis of complex natural products. He currently works as a postdoc at the University of Michigan advised by Tomasz Cierpicki and Jolanta Grembecka.

He is an author of 10+ peer-reviewed papers and 5 international patents in the fields of medicinal chemistry and organic chemistry.

Yiwu Yao was born and raised in Huangshan, Anhui, China. In 2005, he went to the China Pharmaceutical University where he got his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Pharmacy and Medicinal Chemistry, respectively. In 2016, he finished his graduate study and earned his Ph.D. degree in Medicinal Chemistry from the Guangzhou Institutes of Biomedicine and Health, Chinese Academy of Science (GIBH, CAS), mentored by Dr. Sheng Jiang.
