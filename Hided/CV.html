Experience

Grembecka/Cierpicki Lab

Senior Research Fellow, Aug. 2019 - Present
Research Fellow, Aug. 2016 - Jul. 2019

Development of first-in-class small molecule inhibitor targeting PRC1 core component Bmi1-Ring1b E3 ligase.

The Bmi1-Ring1b heterodimer mediate the mono-ubiquitination of H2AK119 via protein-protein interaction with the nucleosome, their abnormal activity has been found to associate with various diseases, including cancers. It is a very challenging to target Bmi1-Ring1b because it has no pocket on the surface.

  1. We identified a hit RB-1 (Kd ~ 7 mM) direct binding to Bmi1-Ring1B using NMR-based fragment screening. Extensive medicinal chemistry campaign gave a lead compound RB-3 (Kd ~ 3 µM) that inhibits H2A ubiquitination in several cancer cell lines and induces differentiation of leukemia cells.
  2. More soluble and potent inhibitors were obtained based on RB-3, and their bound to protein co-crystal structures were solved. With the insight of the detailed binding information, mid-nanomolar binding affinity and low micromolar cellular active inhibitors were rational designed and synthesized. Currently, drug-like refinement of the inhibitor and extensive cell-based studies are ongoing.

Sheng Jiang Lab

PhD, Sep. 2013 – Jun. 2016
Assistant Investigator, Aug. 2012 – Aug. 2013
M.S., Oct. 2010 – Jun. 2012

  • Design, synthesis, and biological evaluation of class selective histone deacetylases inhibitors.

    Histone deacetylases (HDACs) involved in both cancer initiation and cancer progression. Design of class selective HDAC inhibitors are challenging due to the highly conserve deacetylase domains of HDAC family.

    1. Based on FDA approved drug nature product romidepsin (FK228), we designed a series of cyclic depsipeptides HDAC inhibitors which show improved class I HDAC selectivity and reduced toxicity.
    2. Design and synthesis of small molecular HDAC inhibitors using click chemistry, followed by scaffold-hopping lead to compounds demonstrating class I and IIb subtype HDAC selectivity.
  • Total synthesis of biologically active nature products (-)-norsecurinine, (-)-niruroidine and (-)-flueggine A.
  • Designed, synthesized and antitumor activity evaluation two series of dual inhibitors, EGFR/NAMPT and MTH1/NAMPT.
