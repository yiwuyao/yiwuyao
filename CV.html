Research Experience

Grembecka/Cierpicki Lab

Postdoc Research
Aug. 2016 - Present

My project in Cierpicki/Grembecka lab is development of First-In-Class small molecule inhibitor targeting PRC1 core component Ring1B-Bmi1 E3 ligase using fragment-based drug discovery. In this challenging project:

  • I improved the inhibitor binding affinity from micromolar to nanomolar.
  • I developed a convergent and divergent pharmacophore-oriented synthesis route which facilitated the SAR study.
  • Co-crystal structure was obtained using a more soluble and potent inhibitor.
  • Extensive cell-based study is ongoing.

Sheng Jiang Lab

Graduate Research, M.S.&PhD
Oct. 2010 – Jun. 2016
  • Design synthesis, and biological evaluation of cyclic depsipeptide Romidpesin (FK228) analogues and small molecules as anticancer agents selectively targeting Class I HDAC. This project entered preclinical studies.
  • Total synthesis of biologically active nature products (-)-norsecurinine, (-)-niruroidine and (-)-flueggine A.
  • Design, synthesis and antitumor activity evaluation of EGFR/NAMPT dual inhibitors.
  • Practical synthesis of bio-active nature products. (>10 steps)
